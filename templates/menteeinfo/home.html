About PMP
The Placement Mentoring Program seeks to provide mentorship to final-year students in a variety of core and non-core sectors such as finance, analytics, consulting, IT/software, and so on, in order to help them with their placement process. This year’s programme also includes mentorship for the graduating students who wish to pursue higher education. The programme does this through contributions from recently graduated batches who have gone through the similar placement procedure/higher studies preparation and are thus familiar with the problems that may be encountered. The service connects mentors and mentees, allowing the latter to discuss the tactics to use when looking for work in a specific profession. PMP kicks off the initial interaction between the mentors and the mentees, which they may continue at their own pace and according to their requirements, with intermediate follow-ups to address any hurdles that may occur.

What is the further procedure after I register?

The allocation will occur on a first-come, first-served (FCFS) basis. After completing the entire registration process, the allocation process will commence within 2-3 weeks. If you have been assigned a mentor, you will receive mailers from SARC Coordinators. Additionally, in the first 2-3 email interactions, you will need to include us in CC. If you have any questions throughout the process, please feel free to contact us.

What is Grad Mentoring?

Placement Mentoring Program not just focuses on one particular set of graduating students that prioritize placements and their current decision lies in considering going for placements, we also include a considerable chunk of institute students that have intentions in pursuing higher education in various core fields and newer fields like management. Therefore we also include higher education to serve the maximum number of students in the institute.

How did this program benefit mentees last year?

According to reports, the mentees profited greatly from the program last year. The mentoring they received from their mentors was beneficial throughout the placement process. They were able to gain insights from their mentor's experiences, and all of the helpful hints and recommendations assisted them in cracking placements and smoothening the whole process.
